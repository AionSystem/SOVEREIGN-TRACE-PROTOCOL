## I. STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

The Sovereign Trace Protocol exists at the intersection of two
ancient human needs: the need to mark that one was here, and the
need to trust that the mark was not altered after the fact.

This software is infrastructure for epistemic sovereignty —
the individual's right to an unalterable record of their own
existence in time. It is built by a solo architect working outside
institutional systems, without venture capital, without a legal
department, and without the assumption that scale precedes legitimacy.

The code is the argument. The stamp is the proof.
The permanence is the point.

## IV. PUBLIC INTEREST BASIS

The certification registry maintained under the Sovereign Trace
Protocol serves a documented public interest:

1. **AI accountability:** Public documentation of AI system failures
   creates an accountability record that serves the interests of
   users, regulators, and the public in understanding AI system
   performance.

2. **Epistemic transparency:** Immutable audit records prevent
   retroactive alteration of what AI systems said and when —
   a precondition for trustworthy AI governance.

3. **Individual sovereignty:** The personal trace infrastructure
   enables individuals to maintain unalterable records of their
   own existence and actions without dependence on any platform
   or institution.

The public interest basis is relevant to: (a) any GDPR legitimate
interest assessment; (b) any First Amendment / Article 19 analysis;
(c) any fair dealing / fair use analysis of third-party references
to certification findings.
